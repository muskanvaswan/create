when i pass to the afterlife, if it gives me any power over earthly matters —

to design the sky for a day, to tune the twinkling of a star or maybe to angle the growth of plants,

ill pick favorites from the memories of my mortal years and for them id make the sky a little prettier, the stars a little brighter and encourage the plants to grow closer

it will matter then, all the love i distributed to people that didn’t last forever, to my first love or the second, or even the one that was just a conversation.
